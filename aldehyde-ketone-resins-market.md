<p><p>Aldehyde ketone resins are synthetic polymers used in various applications due to their excellent adhesive and finishing properties. The market can be segmented based on polymerization methods: photopolymerization and thermal polymerization. Photopolymerization utilizes light energy to initiate polymerization, allowing for rapid cure times and precise control over the curing process. In contrast, thermal polymerization relies on heat to facilitate the reaction, often resulting in longer curing times but improved mechanical properties. Both methods cater to different industry needs and applications.</p></p>
