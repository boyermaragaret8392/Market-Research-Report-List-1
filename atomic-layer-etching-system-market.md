<p><p>The Atomic Layer Etching System market is primarily categorized into Plasma Type and High Temperature Type systems. Plasma Type systems utilize reactive plasmas to precisely remove material at the atomic level, enabling enhanced control over etch profiles and selectivity. Conversely, High Temperature Type systems leverage elevated temperatures to facilitate etching processes, allowing for the effective removal of materials that may be less responsive to plasma. Both types serve critical roles in advanced semiconductor manufacturing and nanotechnology applications, ensuring high precision and material integrity.</p></p>
