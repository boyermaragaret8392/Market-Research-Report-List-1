<p><p>Automotive bioplastics are bio-based or biodegradable plastics derived from renewable sources such as corn starch, sugarcane, or cellulose. Collaborations between automakers and bioplastic manufacturers are also on the rise, fostering innovation and scalability. As the automotive industry continues to prioritize sustainability, bioplastics are poised to play a pivotal role in shaping the future of eco-friendly vehicle manufacturing.</p></p>
