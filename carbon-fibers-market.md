<p><p>The carbon fiber market is categorized into regular-tow and large-tow carbon fibers. Regular-tow carbon fibers typically have strand counts ranging from 1K to 12K, offering high performance and flexibility for applications in aerospace and sporting goods. In contrast, large-tow carbon fibers, with strand counts exceeding 12K, are designed for industrial applications, providing cost-effectiveness and efficiency in manufacturing. These large-tow fibers are increasingly used in sectors like automotive and renewable energy, where lower material costs are crucial for scalability.</p></p>
