<p><p>The eutectic solder market includes various types based on their composition. Au-Ge (gold-germanium) solder is favored in semiconductor packaging for its stability at high temperatures. Cu-Ag (copper-silver) solder offers good mechanical strength and is often used in electronics and automotive industries.
