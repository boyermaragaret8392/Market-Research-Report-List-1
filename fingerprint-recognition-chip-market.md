<p><strong>The Fingerprint Recognition Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Fingerprint Recognition</li><li>Ultrasonic Fingerprint Recognition</li><li>Optical Fingerprint Recognition</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fingerprint recognition chip market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. **Capacitive Fingerprint Recognition** uses electrical signals to capture fingerprint patterns, providing enhanced accuracy and durability. </p><p>2. **Ultrasonic Fingerprint Recognition** employs high-frequency sound waves to create detailed 3D images of fingerprints, enabling better performance in various conditions, including moisture. </p><p>3. **Optical Fingerprint Recognition** relies on capturing light reflections from the fingerprint's surface, making it cost-effective and widely used in consumer devices, though less effective under certain lighting conditions.</p></p>
