<p><p>The graphite and carbon sealing gasket market features two primary types: pure flexible graphite gaskets and pure flexible graphite-metal inserted gaskets. Pure flexible graphite gaskets are composed entirely of graphite, offering excellent sealing capabilities and temperature resistance, ideal for high-pressure applications. In contrast, pure flexible graphite-metal inserted gaskets combine flexible graphite with a metal insert, enhancing structural integrity and durability while maintaining flexibility. Both types are widely used across industries for their effectiveness in sealing various fluids and gases.</p></p>
