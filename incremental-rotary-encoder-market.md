<p><p>The Incremental Rotary Encoder market is categorized into three main types: mechanical, optical, and magnetic. Mechanical encoders use physical contacts to detect position changes, offering durability but limited resolution. Optical encoders rely on light patterns to determine movement, providing high accuracy and resolution, making them suitable for precision applications. Magnetic encoders utilize magnetic fields and sensors, delivering robustness and resistance to dust and moisture, ideal for harsh environments. Each type offers unique advantages tailored to specific industry needs.</p></p>
