<p><p>The infrared refrigerant leak detector market can be categorized into three main types: CFC leak detectors, HFC leak detectors, and HCFC leak detectors. CFC leak detectors identify leaks from chlorofluorocarbon refrigerants, commonly used in older systems. HFC leak detectors focus on hydrofluorocarbon refrigerants, which are prevalent in modern cooling systems due to their lower ozone depletion potential. HCFC leak detectors target hydrochlorofluorocarbon refrigerants, which serve as transitional substitutes but are being phased out due to their environmental impact. Each detector type caters to specific refrigerant regulations and applications.</p></p>
