<p><ul><li>Controllable Pitch Propeller (CPP)</li><li>Fixed Pitch Propeller (FPP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Marine Thrusters market is primarily categorized into two types: Controllable Pitch Propeller (CPP) and Fixed Pitch Propeller (FPP). CPP systems allow for adjustable blade angles, enhancing maneuverability and efficiency across varying speeds and load conditions, making them ideal for dynamic operations. In contrast, FPPs have a fixed blade angle, offering simplicity and reliability but with limited flexibility. Both types serve essential roles in maritime applications, influencing vessel performance, operational costs, and fuel efficiency in different marine environments.</p></p>
