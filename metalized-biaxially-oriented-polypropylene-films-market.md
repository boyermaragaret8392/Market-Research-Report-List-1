<p><p>Metalized Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) films are categorized into Heat Sealable and Non-Heat Sealable types. Heat Sealable films are coated with a heat-activated layer, enabling them to bond under heat, making them ideal for packaging applications like snacks and confectionery. Non-Heat Sealable films lack this coating and are used in applications requiring high barrier properties, such as labels, laminates, and industrial uses. Both types offer excellent moisture resistance, durability, and aesthetic appeal, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
