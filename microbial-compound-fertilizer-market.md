<p><p>The microbial compound fertilizer market is categorized into three main types: nitrogen-fixing, phosphate-solubilizing, and others. Nitrogen-fixing fertilizers contain microorganisms that convert atmospheric nitrogen into a form accessible to plants, enhancing growth and yield. Phosphate-solubilizing fertilizers improve the bioavailability of phosphorus in soil, promoting root development and crop health. The "others" category encompasses various microbial agents that target specific nutrient deficiencies or enhance soil health, supporting sustainable agriculture practices and improving overall soil fertility.</p></p>
