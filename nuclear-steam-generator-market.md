<p><p>The nuclear steam generator market comprises two main types: horizontal steam generators and vertical steam generators. Horizontal steam generators are designed with horizontal tube arrangements, allowing for efficient heat exchange and maintenance access, making them suitable for various reactor types. Conversely, vertical steam generators feature vertical tube bundles, promoting a compact design with a smaller footprint. Both types serve to transfer heat from reactor coolant to produce steam for turbines, playing a crucial role in nuclear power generation.</p></p>
