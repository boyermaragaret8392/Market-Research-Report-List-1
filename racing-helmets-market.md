<p><p>The racing helmets market comprises three main types: full face, open face, and half helmets. Full face helmets offer maximum protection by covering the entire head and face, ideal for high-speed motorsports. Open face helmets provide ample visibility and ventilation while safeguarding the top and sides of the head, making them popular in less intense racing environments. Half helmets, often seen in casual racing or motorcycle use, offer minimal protection, covering only the skull, thus prioritizing comfort and style over safety.</p></p>
