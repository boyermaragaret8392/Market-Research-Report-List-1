<p><p>Regenerative turbine pumps are categorized into two main types: single-stage and multi-stage. Single-stage regenerative turbine pumps operate with one impeller, making them suitable for applications needing moderate pressure and flow rates. They are efficient for low head requirements. In contrast, multi-stage regenerative turbine pumps consist of multiple impellers, allowing for higher pressure generation and greater flow capacities. They are ideal for applications requiring significant pressure boosts, such as in water supply and industrial processes. Both types are crucial in various industries for effective fluid management.</p></p>
