<p><p>The SCR denitrification catalyst market features three primary types: honeycomb, plate, and corrugated catalysts. Honeycomb catalysts, characterized by their structured channel design, provide efficient gas flow and high surface area for reactions. Plate catalysts, with flat surfaces, are often used in compact applications, offering straightforward manufacturing and installation. Corrugated catalysts boast a wavy structure that enhances surface area while maintaining a compact form, improving gas distribution and reaction efficiency. Each type serves specific needs in reducing nitrogen oxide emissions in various industrial applications.</p></p>
