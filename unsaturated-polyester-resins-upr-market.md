<p><p>Unsaturated Polyester Resins (UPR) are versatile thermosetting polymers widely used in various applications. The UPR market is primarily categorized into two segments: Fiber Reinforced Plastic (FRP) and Non-FRP applications. FRP involves integrating UPR with reinforcing fibers to enhance mechanical properties, making it ideal for construction, automotive, and marine industries. In contrast, the Non-FRP market encompasses UPR usage in coatings, adhesives, and casting materials, where reinforcing fibers are not utilized but maintain desirable chemical and thermal resistance characteristics.</p></p>
