<p><strong>The VoIP Router Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-band</li><li>Dual-band</li><li>Tri-band</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VoIP router market can be categorized into single-band, dual-band, and tri-band types based on their wireless frequency capabilities. Single-band routers operate on the 2.4 GHz frequency, offering basic connectivity suitable for light usage. Dual-band routers support both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz frequencies, providing better performance and reduced interference for more demanding applications like VoIP. Tri-band routers add an additional 5 GHz band, enhancing bandwidth and reducing congestion, making them ideal for high-traffic environments needing reliable voice quality.</p></p>
