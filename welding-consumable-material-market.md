Stick electrodes are versatile and used for manual welding processes. Solid wires are commonly used in MIG welding for solid metal connections. Flux-cored wires offer better protection against contamination and are suitable for outdoor use. SAW wires and fluxes are utilized for high-volume, deep welds in industrial applications, providing high efficiency and quality.</p></p>
